Alfredo Fettuccine Pasta

Ingredients

  • 1 pound fettuccine pasta
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 pint heavy cream
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

How to Prepare Alfredo Fettuccine Pasta!

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add fettuccini and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
  2. In a large saucepan, melt butter into cream over low heat. Add salt, pepper and nutmeg. Stir in Parmesan cheese over medium heat until melted; this will thicken the sauce.
  3. Add pasta to sauce. Use enough of the pasta so that all of the sauce is used and the pasta is thoroughly coated. Serve immediately.
